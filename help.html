• For Launching The Pods:
    1. use keywords launch/create/make and pod/pods
    2. give pod name after the keyword name/name is
  • For Running Deployment:
    1. use keywords launch/create/make and deployment
    2. give deployment image name after the keyword image/name is
  • For Expose Services:
    1. use keywords expose and deployment
    2. give the port no. after the keyword 'port'
  • For Scaling Replica:
    1. use keywords increase/decrease/scale/replicate/replica
    2. give the no. of replica after the keyword 'to'
  • For deleting the services and environment:
    1. use keywords 'delete'
